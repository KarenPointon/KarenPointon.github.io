About me

Karen Pointon

Hi, I’m Karen. I’ve been a professional photographer and animation producer since 2006. Throughout this time I have worked with a range of clients including Poole Pottery, Urban Vision, Appetite Stoke, Staffordshire Housing Association and a host of artists and creative individuals who need to showcase their work.

Every project I have worked on has it’s own unique story and that’s exactly what I aim to capture.
